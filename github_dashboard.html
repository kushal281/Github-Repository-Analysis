Conclusion

Repo age is the strongest linear predictor of stars — older projects have had more time to accumulate community. However, some 2025 repos already sit at 350K+ stars, showing viral AI projects break this pattern. Forks correlate most strongly with stars (r=0.608) but both are outcomes of popularity, not drivers. A non-linear model (Random Forest) is the recommended next step.
